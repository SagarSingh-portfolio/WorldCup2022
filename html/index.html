What is the World Cup?

The World Cup is the largest event held ever. It is a sporting competition held every 4 years to crown the best country in Football. The tournament contains 32 Senoir men's teams, split into 8 groups of 4 teams where a round robin is played. The top 2 teams of each group advance into the last 16 round. The Last 16 is where the traditional tournament style begins where its sudden death, losing results in Elimination.

This year the World Cup is taking place in Qatar as its hosts. The event begins on November 20th and ends on December 18th at the Lusail Stadium. This is 22nd World Cup in History. All matches can be viewed freely from national broadcasting services.

FIFA is the organization that governs the World Cup events. The event brings viewers from every country uniting them for one cause. Following your own country or any country you are a fan is a pride to support them. From big upsets to incredible individual performances, the World Cup has the most intense and entertaining matches.

Brazil have won a record 5 times the World Cup. Germany and Italy have 4 wins each at second most. Current champions are France with their win in Russia 2018 with complete domination in the tournament. Could they do it again and be the third country to win it back to back?

Argentina are going into the tournaments as the favorites to win it all. They have a 36 unbeaten run (1 off from tieing the record) and won Copa America and Finalisma recently. Are Messi's dreams of winning the World Cup going to be achieved?
